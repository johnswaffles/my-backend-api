# Cozy Village rebuild

Clean Godot 4.6 rebuild of the relaxing neighborhood builder. The former Cozy
Builder project remains intact and is not a dependency.

## Product pillars

1. A beautiful miniature neighborhood at first glance.
2. Place a small number of meaningful things; avoid management overload.
3. Residents move with weight, rhythm, and purpose rather than sliding.
4. Warm music and environmental motion make idle time pleasant.
5. Expand only after the visual slice meets the approved concept target.

The approved art-direction image is stored at
`assets/concepts/cozy-village-art-target-v1.png`.
